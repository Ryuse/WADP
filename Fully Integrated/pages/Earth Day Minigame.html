Forest Fire Run!


In this game, you are a runner who has discovered a raging forest fire in the forest near your home. Trees are burning and you must avoid getting burned or the game is over. Use the up and down keys to navigate the runner through the forest. Collect trash as you progress to score bonus points. How far can you get before you get burned down?
